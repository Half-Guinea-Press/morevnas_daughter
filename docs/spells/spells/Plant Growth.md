# Plant Growth
3rd-level Transmutation<br>
Casting Time:1 action or 8 hours<br>
Range: 150 feet<br>
Duration: Instantaneous<br>
Component: V, S

Spell Description:<br>
This spell channels vitality into plants within a specific area. There are two possible uses for the spell, granting either immediate or long-term benefits.<br>If you cast this spell using 1 action, choose a point within range. All normal plants in a 100-foot radius centered on that point become thick and overgrown. A creature moving through the area must spend 4 feet of movement for every 1 foot it moves.<br>You can exclude one or more areas of any size within the spellâ€™s area from being affected.<br>If you cast this spell over 8 hours, you enrich the land. All plants in a half-mile radius centered on a point within range become enriched for 1 year. The plants yield twice the normal amount of food when harvested.
